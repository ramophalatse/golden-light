About Dineo Maboe-Khambule

I am a Tshwane based Clinical Psychologist registered with the Health Professionals Council of South Africa (HPCSA) and Board of Healthcare Funders of Southern Africa (BHF).

I completed my BA (General), BA Honours in Psychology and Masters in Clinical Psychology degrees at the University of South Africa (UNISA).

I belong to the African Spirituality and Psychoanalytic Self Psychology Group. I provide input on African spirituality, Psychology and way of life.

Modalities of practice

I practice in different modalities, providing flexibility as it pertains to the presenting issue and relevant scope of intervention. I am trained in:

  • Brainspotting
  • Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)
  • Interactional Patterns of Analysis (IPA)
  • Integrated Brain Based Approach (IBBA)
  • Systematic psychotherapy

I also have a keen interest in African Epistemology and Psychology, as well as topics of interest such as traditional healing, cultural rites, traditions and practices.

I am passionate about community work and provide sessional services for the State.

I offer face to face sessions and virtual sessions through Doxy.me and other virtual platforms.
